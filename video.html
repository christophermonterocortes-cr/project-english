Technical English Subarea — Benefits & Profile

The English Oriented to Web Development subarea is a key component of the Web Development specialty at CTP La Carpio. It equips students with the technical vocabulary, reading comprehension skills, and professional communication strategies required to thrive in an international tech environment.

Throughout this subarea, students learn to read and interpret official documentation, collaborate on platforms such as GitHub, participate in stand-up meetings, and present technical projects confidently in English. The goal is to prepare graduates who can work seamlessly in remote teams, freelance for international clients, and pursue continuous learning through global tech resources.
